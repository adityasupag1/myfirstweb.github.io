OUR FREEDOM FIGHTERS

India got its independence on the 15th day of August of 1947, this day is the result of the freedom struggle fought by our great heroes. A series of struggles, movements, battles, and uprisings took place to make India independent through the contribution of great freedom fighters. Great freedom fighter such as Bal Gangadhar Tilak, Dr. Rajendra Prasad, Dr. Lal Bhadur Shastri, Sardar Vallabh Bhai Patel, Mahatma Gandhi, Jawahar Lal Nehru, and other freedom fighter has contributed a lot. There is no parameter to identify the top 10 Freedom Fighters of India. Every Freedom Fighter in India has played a major role to got freedom to the country and sacrificing their life. Here, we are sharing the list of freedom fighters of India and their massive contribution to the country's struggle for getting Independence for India after a lot of effort.

Bal Gangadhar Tilak

Bal Gangadhar Tilak, also known as Lokmanya Tilak, was a prominent Indian freedom fighter, nationalist, social reformer, and writer who played a significant role in India's struggle for independence from British rule. He was born on July 23, 1856, in Ratnagiri, Maharashtra, India, and passed away on August 1, 1920.

Dr. Rajendra Prasad

Dr. Rajendra Prasad, often referred to as Rajendra Babu, was an Indian political leader, lawyer, and statesman who played a significant role in India's struggle for independence from British rule. While he is primarily known for his contribution to the Indian independence movement, he also made significant contributions to the shaping of India as its first President.

Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi, also known as Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, was a prominent figure in the Indian independence movement and a renowned leader of nonviolent resistance. He is often referred to as the "Father of the Nation" in India. Born on October 2, 1869, in Porbandar, Gujarat, India, Gandhi played a crucial role in India's struggle for freedom from British colonial rule.
